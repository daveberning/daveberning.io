# About Me

I'm Dave Berning, a Front-End Software Engineer based in Cincinnati, OH. I specialize in building design systems and creating accessible user experiences. I care deeply about the craft of front-end development and the people who use the things I build.

## How I Build

I believe in minimizing dependencies. You don't always need an NPM package — sometimes building something unique for your project is better long-term than reaching for an all-in-one, out-of-the-box solution. When you own the code, you understand it, and understanding what you ship matters.

## How I Collaborate

I don't claim to have all the answers. I do my best work when I'm formulating solutions within a group — bouncing ideas around, pressure-testing assumptions, and building something better than any one person would have come up with alone. Collaboration isn't a buzzword for me; it's how I actually prefer to work.
